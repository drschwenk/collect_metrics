Find errors in the video
1. Two questions are asked about the contents of the video.
2. Click on the words that are missing or false for each question.
3. If everything is correct for a question, click its "None Missing" button.
4. To undo a selection, just click the word again.
5. If you're unsure whether something is correct, you should mark it as incorrect.
6. You only need to worry about words that are highlighted yellow for each question.
7. Some of the videos might look strange or distorted- just do your best to identify the issues present.
